# When the next meeting won't bring an answer you don't already have.

At some point, every internal discussion runs out of road. The numbers are unclear, opinions differ, the decision gets postponed. Everyone has a view – no one has facts.

That's the moment for an outside perspective.

Two services. Defined timeframe. Concrete output. No ongoing mandate.

---

## What we offer

---

### Project Review

A project that isn't delivering as planned – or one where you don't want to make a critical decision based on gut feeling.

We analyse the current state: numbers, data, facts from project reporting, backlog, and risk position. Supplemented by interviews and workshops with the people involved – because what's in the reports is rarely the full picture.

The result: a final report with a clear catalogue of measures. No opinions without foundation. No report that nobody reads.

---

### Pilot Delivery

You have an idea – an automation, an AI application, a new process solution. Before you commission a full project, you want to know: does this actually work? What does it actually cost? What are the real risks?

We build the pilot – with speed and a single focus: real results instead of assumptions. The output isn't a presentation. It's working software you can evaluate firsthand.

Along with that: clear recommendations and conclusions. What makes the case for the full project? What needs to be resolved first? Which risks are manageable – and which aren't? You set the right course before the real investment begins.

---

## Why this approach

Most consulting engagements end with recommendations. What happens next is up to the client.

Our approach is different: defined scope, clear deliverables, direct handover of the decision-making basis. No gradual transition into the next engagement. No dependency.

You stay in control. We deliver the facts.
