## Hosting and Server Log Files

This website is hosted by **GitHub Pages**. The provider automatically collects information in so-called server log files, which your browser automatically transmits: browser type and version, operating system, referrer URL, hostname of the accessing computer, time of the server request, and IP address.

This data is not merged with other data sources. The legal basis is Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR (legitimate interest in the technically error-free presentation of the website).

## Cookies

This website uses **technically necessary cookies** that are required for the operation of the site. They do not store personal data and are not used for tracking purposes.

A cookie storing your cookie settings is set on your first visit (`cc_cookie`, duration: 6 months). The legal basis is Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR.

Analytical cookies are set **exclusively with your explicit consent** (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR). You can withdraw your consent at any time via the cookie settings button at the bottom of the page.
