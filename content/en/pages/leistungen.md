Complex IT and AI projects produce the same situations, again and again.
The questions differ – but the moment they arise is familiar: when what is available internally is no longer enough.

Three services. Each for a concrete situation.

---

## Your team needs reinforcement – someone who takes responsibility.

A high-stakes AI initiative. Your internal project managers know the subject matter – but the combination of stakeholder management, agile delivery control and risk oversight exceeds available capacity. What's missing isn't another advisor on the sidelines. What's missing is someone who actually takes responsibility for delivery and outcome.

**Interim Management** means: operational project leadership, full accountability, direct reporting line to the steering committee. For as long as the initiative requires.

[More on Interim Management →](/interim-management/)

---

## Your project is running – but something isn't right.

The status report shows green. The mood in the team shows something else. Milestones are being met – but whether the outcome will actually deliver what the budget was approved for, nobody is sure anymore.

Doubts like these don't arise without reason. And they don't go away if you wait.

A **Project Review** provides clarity: is the project where it should be? What are the real risks – not the reported ones? What measures are needed to bring the initiative back on track? The result is a concrete action catalogue – not a report nobody reads.

[To the Project Review →](/consulting/#project-review)

---

## You want to start – but not invest into the unknown.

An initiative is taking shape. The idea is clear, the budget not yet approved – or not yet quantified. What's missing are answers to the real questions: is this technically feasible the way we're imagining it? What will it actually cost? And what risks do we need to understand before we commit?

A **Pilot** delivers exactly that: not a presentation, but working software – in a defined scope, with the sole purpose of creating the decision basis for the full project.

[To the Pilot →](/consulting/#pilot-delivery)
