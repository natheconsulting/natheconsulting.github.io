You build. With modern frameworks, LLMs, RAG architectures – and with the understanding that working software isn't automatically useful software.

You work iteratively, value feedback from the business side, and can explain technical decisions in a way a project manager actually understands. No ivory tower. No code nobody can operate.

## What you bring

- Hands-on experience with LLM applications, RAG systems or ML pipelines
- Knowledge of modern AI frameworks and cloud infrastructure (e.g. AWS, Azure)
- Agile mindset and willingness to show results early and openly
- Understanding of business requirements – not just technical feasibility

## What to expect

Projects with genuine complexity – with clients who take the technology seriously and demand the value. Technical decisions you help shape. No staffing into arbitrary tickets.
