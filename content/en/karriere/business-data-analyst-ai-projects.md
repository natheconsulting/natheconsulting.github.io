You translate. Between what the business needs and what the data can support. Between what a model can do and what a process can absorb.

You know BPMN, can structure requirements – and you know when a data foundation is good enough for an AI initiative and when it isn't. You ask the questions others don't dare to.

## What you bring

- Experience in requirements elicitation and documentation, classical and agile
- Knowledge of process modelling (BPMN) and data analysis
- Understanding of how ML models are trained and what data requirements that entails
- Ability to present findings in a way that enables decisions

## What to expect

Projects where your analysis feeds directly into product decisions. No layers of reports nobody reads. A small, experienced team – close to the client, close to the outcome.
